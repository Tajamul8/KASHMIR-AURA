About Kashmir Aura

Welcome to Kashmir Aura, your trusted travel partner for exploring the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir. Founded and managed by Tajamul, Kashmir Aura is dedicated to providing unforgettable travel experiences with professional service, comfort, and affordable packages.

We provide customized Kashmir tour packages including Srinagar, Gulmarg, Sonamarg, Pahalgam, Doodhpathri, and many hidden paradise destinations across the valley. Our services include hotel booking, cab services, honeymoon tours, family trips, adventure tours, sightseeing, and complete travel planning.

At Kashmir Aura, our mission is to show the real beauty, culture, hospitality, and peaceful environment of Kashmir to tourists from all over India and abroad.
